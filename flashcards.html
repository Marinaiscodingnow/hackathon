Study Tips

~Write stuff down (writing down important notes can help you memorize them better.)

~Be prepared (You want to have everything you need to succeed).

~Break up your studying times (Don't study all day, give your brain breaks to allow it to come back fresh)

~Try a different setting (Getting fresh air and allowing your body to move can increase brain activity)

~Make it fun! (Make studying something to look forward to, not dread)
